my story

From a young age I have been passionate about computer games - always PC! This passion developed over the years and led me to explore the world of software development, as a result I taught myself simple coding as a teenager.

Whilst I never lost my passion for computers, my working career to date has been within the catering industry. I have developed a multitude of transferable skills over my time working as a front of house team member. To list a few, I can work effectively as part of a team, I can communicate with a wide variety of individuals and I am motivated and enthusiastic towards any challenge I face.

I have reached a point in my working life where I want my career to reflect my passions. As a result I enrolled on an online software development diploma which I am driven to complete alongside working full time in catering. My current goal is to build a career that utilises not only the coding knowledge I am gaining daily but the transferrable skills I have honed over the past 10 years.
